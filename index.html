Meet Remembra

A unique platform to connect music to memories. Designed to bring comfort, connection, and a touch of the familiar, it’s music therapy reimagined.

Use Cases

Alzheimer’s & Dementia Care

Remembra helps individuals with memory loss reconnect with cherished moments through personalized music therapy. Caregivers can use the app to create soothing, familiar soundscapes that reduce anxiety, improve mood, and enhance engagement.

Concussion & Brain Injury Recovery

Music has been shown to aid cognitive function and emotional regulation after traumatic brain injuries. Remembra adapts to each user’s needs, providing calming or stimulating playlists that support mental recovery and focus.

Wellness & Stress Relief

Beyond clinical care, Remembra serves as a tool for relaxation, mindfulness, and emotional well-being. Whether managing daily stress or seeking comfort through familiar melodies, users can experience the therapeutic power of music anytime, anywhere.
